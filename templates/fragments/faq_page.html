Frequently Asked Questions

What is Podmem?

Podmem is a spaced repetition and flashcard generator platform designed to help remember the things that we hear.

How do I change my email address or password?

I’ve forgotten or lost my password

If you've forgotten your password, there is currently no option to retrieve it. We recommend that you make a new user, until we have made updates to the app.

I’ve forgotten or lost my email address

If you've forgotten the email address associated with your account, please contact us and provide as many details as possible, including your username and any alternative email addresses you may have used.

How do I contact you?

Flashcards from YouTube

How can I export my flashcards to work with other flashcard apps?

You can export your flashcards by following these steps:

  1. Paste YouTube link
  2. Click the export button.
  3. Choose the desired format, such as Anki or other apps.
For specific instructions on importing into your chosen app, we recommend searching online for guidance tailored to that app's requirements.

Do you support other languages?

Currently, Podmem only supports English. However, we are actively working to include support for other languages in the near future.

Can I use the content generated for educational or commercial purposes?

Yes, all content generated by Podmem, including flashcards, is free to use for both educational and commercial purposes.

Are my flashcards private and secure?

Yes, your flashcards are securely stored on our servers. When you create a deck, it remains private and is not shared publicly on our website or with other users.
